# Touchpoint Guide

## What Are Touchpoints?

**Touchpoints** are strategic interactions with prospects designed to build familiarity, trust, and engagement over time. Each touchpoint is a planned contact through various channels to stay top-of-mind without being pushy.

The 7th Level Dialer uses a **30-day, 27-touch sequence** that combines multiple channels to create a persistent but professional outreach campaign.

---

## Why Multiple Touchpoints?

Research shows that:
- It takes 7-13+ touches to generate a qualified lead
- Multi-channel approaches increase response rates by 300%
- Consistent, spaced-out engagement builds trust better than aggressive bursts
- Different people prefer different communication channels

Our sequence ensures you're reaching prospects through their preferred channels while maintaining a professional cadence.

## Tracking Touchpoints

Each completed touchpoint updates the contact's engagement counters:
- `calls_made` - Total phone calls made
- `voicemails_left` - Voicemails left (subset of calls)
- `emails_sent` - Emails sent
- `linkedin_dms_sent` - LinkedIn DMs sent
- `linkedin_comments_made` - LinkedIn comments posted
- `social_reactions` - Social media likes/reactions
- `social_comments` - Social media comments
- `physical_mail_sent` - Postcard mailed (boolean)

These metrics help you understand:
- How many touches it typically takes to convert
- Which channels perform best
- Where prospects engage most
- When to mark a lead as dead

## Best Practices for Success

### 1. **Personalize Every Touch**
- Reference their company, role, or recent activity
- Avoid generic templates
- Make each touch feel human

### 2. **Respect Rest Days**
- The built-in rest days prevent overwhelming prospects
- Use these days for research and preparation
- Never compress the sequence to speed it up

### 3. **Track Everything**
- Log every touchpoint completion immediately
- Add notes about responses or observations
- Use data to optimize future sequences

### 4. **Provide Value**
- Every email should educate or help
- LinkedIn comments should add insight
- Avoid pitchy, salesy language

### 5. **Be Patient**
- Some prospects need all 27 touches before responding
- Decision-makers are busy - persistence shows commitment
- Trust the process

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change the sequence order?**
A: The sequence is optimized based on sales research, but you can customize `SEQUENCE_CALENDAR` in `/src/lib/sequenceCalendar.js` if needed.

**Q: What if they respond mid-sequence?**
A: Pause the sequence immediately and engage in conversation. Use the "Convert to Client" action if they become a customer.

**Q: Should I personalize every touchpoint?**
A: Yes! Templates are a starting point, but personalization dramatically increases response rates.

**Q: What if I don't have their LinkedIn or social media?**
A: The system automatically skips those tasks. Focus on the channels you do have access to.

**Q: How do I know when to mark someone dead?**
A: After completing the 30-day sequence with no response, it's reasonable to mark them as dead. Consider waiting 90 days before retrying.
